### Loss Function in Neural Network

You must have heard of “loss function” in Neural Networks, but do you exactly know what it is? What is the meaning of it?

Let me tell you in a quite simple way:

Loss functions are those functions that are used to calculate the overall difference between the actual output and the predicted output, and also apply some mathematical formulas to make further calculations easier.
